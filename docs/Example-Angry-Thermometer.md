# Example emotion thermometer — Angry

Translated from `examples/Voorbeeld thermometer boos.docx`.

| Zone | Feeling | Physical signals | Thoughts | Behavior | What can I do? |
|------|---------|------------------|----------|----------|----------------|
| **Red** | Rage outburst | Increased heart rate | If I see him now, I’ll hit him! | Shouting, kicking, hitting | Take a short break away from others |
| **Red** | Furious | Adrenaline rush, clenched fists | Everyone is against me! | Freezing, crying fits | Move vigorously |
| **Orange** | Angry, impatient | Faster breathing | This can’t be happening?! | Raising my voice | Go cycling, drink tea |
| **Orange** | Irritated | Tense jaw, overactive | Everything is going wrong! | Restless behavior, stomping | Explain why I’m angry |
| **Yellow** | Slightly irritated | Tension in body | I find this annoying | Worrying, fretting | State my boundaries |
| **Green** | Calm | Relaxed | I feel good | Tidy, taking care of myself | Enjoy the day |

---

## Color guide

| Color | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Red | Very angry; body and thoughts are in overdrive |
| Orange | Clearly angry; still possible to slow down with help |
| Yellow | Mild irritation; good time to speak up or take space |
| Green | Calm and regulated |
