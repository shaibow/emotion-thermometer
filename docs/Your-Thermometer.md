# Your emotion thermometer (fill in)

Use this blank template to build your own thermometer. Add one row per color zone (red → orange → yellow → green). You can focus on any emotion (anger, fear, sadness, joy, etc.).

**Columns**

| Column | What to write |
|--------|----------------|
| **Zone** | Color level: Red, Orange, Yellow, or Green |
| **Feeling** | Name of the feeling at this level |
| **Physical signals** | What you notice in your body |
| **Thoughts** | Typical thoughts at this level |
| **Behavior** | What you tend to do |
| **What can I do?** | Helpful actions or coping strategies |

## Notes

- **Red** = very intense; hard to think clearly  
- **Orange** = strong emotion; still manageable with support  
- **Yellow** = mild discomfort; good moment to act early  
- **Green** = calm; you feel okay and regulated
