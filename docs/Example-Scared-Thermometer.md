# Example emotion thermometer — Scared

Translated from `examples/Voorbeeld Thermometer.docx` (fear section).

| Zone | Feeling | Physical signals | Thoughts | Behavior | What can I do? |
|------|---------|------------------|----------|----------|----------------|
| **Red** | Panic | Panic attack | I won’t survive this | Crying, hyperventilating | Take a short break away from others |
| **Red** | Very scared | Short of breath | I have to get out of here | Freezing, crying fits | Look for a distraction |
| **Orange** | Afraid | Clammy hands | I don’t want this; this is scary | Fleeing | Drink water |
| **Orange** | A little afraid | Tension, tired, or overactive | It’s becoming too much for me | Restless behavior or very quiet | Talk about it with someone |
| **Yellow** | Nervous | Tension in body | This is exciting | Nervous, worrying | Listen to music |
| **Green** | Comfortable in my skin | Relaxed | I feel good | Tidy, taking care of myself | Enjoy the day |

---

## Color guide

| Color | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Red | Overwhelming fear; fight/flight/freeze is strong |
| Orange | Clear fear; coping strategies can still help |
| Yellow | Mild nerves; good moment to prepare or ask for support |
| Green | Calm and safe enough in your body |
